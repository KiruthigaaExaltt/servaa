Prompt 16: Crew Management & Staff Operations
Objective: Create the CrewManagement.tsx component to handle employee profiles, shift scheduling, real-time attendance, and payroll summaries.

"Create a new component CrewManagement.tsx to manage the restaurant’s human resources.

1. Staffing KPIs:

Total Staff: (e.g., 24 Active).

On-Duty Now: (e.g., 8 clocked in).

Labor Cost %: (Labor cost vs. Total Sales).

Shift Gaps: (Alerts if a specific zone, like 'Garden', has no waiter assigned).

2. Employee Directory (The 'Crew' List):

Build a searchable grid or table of all staff members.

Card/Row Details: >   - Profile: Small avatar, Name, and Employee ID.

Role Badge: (e.g., Admin, Manager, Head Chef, Waiter, Rider).

Status Badge: 'On Duty' (Green pulse), 'Off Duty' (Gray), or 'On Leave' (Amber).

Quick Stats: Performance rating (stars) and Total hours this week.

3. Shift & Roster Management:

Create a 'Weekly Roster' view.

Functionality: A simple calendar-style grid showing days of the week and 'Shift Blocks' (e.g., Morning: 8 AM - 4 PM, Evening: 4 PM - 12 AM).

Allow the manager to click a block to assign a staff member to a specific 'Zone' (e.g., 'Anil assigned to Main Hall - Evening Shift').

4. Attendance & Live Punch-In:

Add a 'Live Attendance' tab showing exactly what time each staff member clocked in.

Include a 'Manual Punch' button for managers to correct errors.

Show the current 'Session Duration' for everyone on the floor.

5. Financials & Payouts:

Earnings Summary: A view showing Base Salary + Total Tips earned (linked from the FOHManagement Tip ledger).

Payout Status: Toggle between 'Paid' and 'Unpaid' for the current month.

Include a 'Generate Payslip' button that creates a summary of their hours and earnings.

6. Role-Based Permissions (Settings):

A sub-section to define what each role can see (e.g., 'Waiters can see KOTs but not Inventory Prices')."

Why this is the "Team Engine":
The Tip Loop: This finally connects the tips collected in the FOH module to the actual person's monthly earnings.

Operational Safety: By seeing "On-Duty Now," a manager can quickly spot if they are understaffed before the dinner rush starts.

Accountability: Role-based permissions ensure that sensitive financial data stays with the Admin while the Waiters stay focused on the guests.
